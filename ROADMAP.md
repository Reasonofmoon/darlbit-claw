# 🌕 Reason of Moon (달의 이성) Platform Roadmap

> **Mission**: Transforming "OpenClaw" from a generic CLI agent into a **Reflective Intelligence System** powered by Moonlight Thinking.

## 1. 🧬 Philosophy: Moonlight Thinking (야간 비행)
Unlike the generic "CMDSPACE" which focuses on functional utility, **Reason of Moon** focuses on **Depth & Reflection**.
-   **Core Identity**: The "Moon" represents the reflective self—analyzing data in the quiet of the night to produce wisdom (Meaning).
-   **Agent Persona**: **Moltbot (Darlbot)** is not just a tool, but a "Partner in Thought" that evolves through the **Oblivion Chamber** (learning from failure) and **Living Grimoire** (accumulating skills).
